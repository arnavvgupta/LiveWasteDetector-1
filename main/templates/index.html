Waste Monitorng Solution

Our Waste Monitoring solution allows cities and businesses to uncover and understand the complex dynamics of their waste production. The solution provides real-time online access to data related to monitored bins, boxes, or containers.


The Models use Machine Learning technology to measure the contents in bins several times a day. The Model send the data to Governement Waste Management System, a powerful cloud-based platform, for data-driven daily operations. Customers are hence provided data-driven decision making, and optimization of waste collection routes, frequencies, and vehicle loads resulting in route reduction by at least 30%.
